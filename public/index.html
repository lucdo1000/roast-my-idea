RoastMyIdea — Analysis
"An app that lets you rate and review your Uber drivers before the trip ends so they get real-time feedback."

Verdict

Needs serious rethinking

Uber already has a post-trip rating system — you're describing a feature request, not a startup. Real-time ratings during a ride create safety concerns Uber's legal team would never allow, and any driver who knew they were being rated mid-trip would either drive erratically or game the system immediately. The "market" here is one API call away from being owned by Uber.

What could work: Shift to independent contractors (DoorDash, TaskRabbit) where platform ratings lag significantly. Build a reputation layer that works across platforms. That's a real gap.
